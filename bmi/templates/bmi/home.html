Welcome to the BMI Calculator

The BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a simple tool to help you determine if you have a healthy body weight for your height. It provides a numerical value that falls into various categories to help assess your body weight status.

To use the calculator, simply enter your weight and height into the appropriate fields, and the calculator will compute your BMI. Based on your BMI, you'll receive information on whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

If you need personalized suggestions based on your BMI category, you can click on the "Suggestion" button on the Calculate BMI page after obtaining your BMI result.

BMI Categories

BMI Range Category
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30 and above Obesity
